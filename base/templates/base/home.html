Less is More

We are tired of bloated "enterprise" wikis that try to do everything and isn't pleasant to use. suppleText is better because we do less. We focus on a small number of features but strive to do them very well.

Enhanced WikiCreole Syntax

Editing wiki pages is a breeze with Creole, a standard wiki markup language. Our enhancements allow you to directly add HTML and style markup along with wiki text for maximum flexibility.

Everyone Already Has An Account

Visitors to your wiki can easily contribute without needing to tediously create an account. They can login with their existing Google, Facebook, Twitter, Yahoo, Microsoft, or OpenID accounts in a few clicks.

History, Revisions, and Comparisons

All changes to wiki documents are tracked (along with author and date) and stored in revisions. Our unlimited history of changes enable you to compare revisions and rollback changes to any point in time.

Administrative Control Panel

Easily manage your wiki settings and users with a control panel accessible to wiki administrators. You can add contributors to your wiki and change global page permissions. Additional features in progress!

Contributors and Permissions

Permissions enable you to control who can view and edit pages, from anyone to logged in users and special designated contributors. The flexibility in permissions allows you, for instance, to run a private wiki.

Feedback and Constant Improvements

We really listen to your feedback and work on improving suppleText constantly, with changes pushed out every day. Your feedback will help determine what features we add.
